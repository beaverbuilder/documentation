The Welcome tab offers quick access to resources for getting started with Beaver Builder. You can create a new page, see what’s new, and find links to support and community resources.

## Access the Welcome tab

1. Access your site's WordPress Admin Dashboard.
2. Navigate to **Settings > Beaver Builder**.
3. Click the **Welcome** tab.

<Warning>
  The Beaver Builder Welcome settings page cannot be white labeled and is automatically removed when Branding options are enabled.
</Warning>

## Welcome Options

The Welcome tab includes the following options:

### Getting Started

The Getting Started section is designed for new users, offering a quick way to create a new page and begin building their first layout with Beaver Builder.

### What’s New

The What’s New section highlights the latest features and enhancements in your installed version of Beaver Builder. It also includes links to the update post, changelog, and a YouTube video overview of the release.

### Help & Share

The Help & Share section connects you with the Beaver Builder community and support resources. You can join user groups on Facebook, Slack, Discord, or the Forums to share ideas and ask questions, and access the Knowledge Base or support links for help and updates.
